KAI Mograph

This project focuses on animating the official KAI (Kereta Api Indonesia) logo using motion graphic techniques. The main goal is to present a more modern and dynamic brand identity through smooth transitions, simple visual effects, and consistent use of KAI’s color scheme (blue, orange, and white). The animation is designed for use in digital branding, video openings, and promotional content.

Role

  • Motion Graphic Designer

Technologi/Software

  • Alight Motion

Key Features

  • Smooth transition effects
  • Clean, minimalist, and modern animation style
  • Consistent with KAI brand colors and identity
  • Adaptable for use in intro/outro videos

Results

  • A short motion graphic video highlighting KAI's brand identity
  • professional and visually appealing animation
  • Ready to use for promotional or corporate content
